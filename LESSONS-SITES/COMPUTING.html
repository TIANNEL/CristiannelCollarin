The Computing Industry

Definitions

  • Computing: Any goal-oriented activity requiring or benefiting from computer machinery.
  • Computer Science: Deals with the theory of computation or design of computers, relying on testable explanations.
  • Information Technology (IT): Technology involving development, use of computer systems, and distribution of data.
  • Entertainment and Multimedia: Interdisciplinary field integrating computer science and creative arts.

Theoretical Foundations

  • Theory of Computation: Investigates the efficiency of problem-solving on a model of computation using algorithms.
  • Three Great Insights:
    • Computers represent anything with two objects.
    • Computers perform anything using five actions.
    • Computers combine actions in only three ways.

Industry in the Philippines

  • Information Economy: Includes ICT manufacturing, trade, services, and content/media sectors.
  • Utilization: In 2021, 83.5% of ICT-enabled services employees routinely used computers.
  • Growth: Mobile phone transactions for businesses grew 133.1% from 2017 to 2019.
  • Employment: The ICT services sector employed 752,142 workers in 2021.

Career Opportunities

  • IT Focus: Professionals manipulate, store, retrieve, and broadcast data while managing information ethics.
  • Research Scientists: Design innovative uses for new technology.
  • Security Analysts: Plan and carry out measures to protect networks.
  • Software Developers: Design computer applications or programs.
  • Database Architects: Create systems to store and secure data.
  • Web Developers: Create and maintain websites.
